Why we exist

Our Mission

At Aura Green, we are on an unyielding mission to accelerate India's transition to sustainable energy. Leveraging cutting-edge technologies, we facilitate the deployment of renewable energy solutions from CBG manufacturing plants to solar rooftops and Green Diesel outlets. Our comprehensive end-to-end services empower entrepreneurs, enrich local communities, and contribute to a cleaner, healthier environment. Together, we're not just meeting the energy demands of today; we're paving the way for a more sustainable, energy-efficient tomorrow.

What we do

Embrace a new era of sustainability with Aura Green. We are more than an EPC company; we are your partners in creating a cleaner, brighter, and more sustainable tomorrow. From CBG plants to solar solutions, we guide entrepreneurs in building the green enterprises of the future.

Compressed Bio Gas (Bio-CNG)

We do transforming organic waste into clean, zero-carbon energy. Partner with us for end-to-end excellence in CBG production and become an entrepreneur of change in India's green revolution.

Diesel to CNG
Conversion for Tractors

Revolutionize agricultural economics with our high fuel-efficient, low-cost, and eco-friendly CNG kits, saving farmers at least Rs 1 Lac annually. Choose a customized, safe, and environmentally beneficial solution tailored to your tractor's HP and engine capacity.

Green Diesel
Outlets

Experience the future of eco-friendly fueling with our Green Diesel, derived from renewable feedstocks for a reduced carbon footprint and seamless performance. Trust in our rigorous quality standards as you join us in driving towards a sustainable future.

Solar Power Solutions

Harness the power of the sun for optimal returns and minimal maintenance, with custom solar solutions tailored for all—from industries to farms. Turn unused rooftops and unproductive land into sustainable energy sources and start your solar journey with Aura Power Solar today.

Rain Water Hervesting

Elevate your water conservation efforts with our self-cleaning, high-efficiency Dual Intensity rooftop filters and affordable solutions. Experience innovative groundwater replenishment through our cutting-edge injection well technology, tailored for comprehensive rainwater harvesting.
